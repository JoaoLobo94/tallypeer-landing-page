Managed OpenSource Cooperation made profitable

Bringing money into open source's passion

Our technology makes hiring any kind of work a thing of the past, the people that will work for your project, will do so because of your vision, while getting payed. The days of coding for a passion with no return but the feeling of helping the community are over. With our managed services, you can focus on creating the best projects. We will deal with attracting developers for your project, generating sales, and paying those developers, with the power of bitcoin.

Managed services

We offer complete managed services for your project. You will talk with one of our representatives, and he will understand what are your needs, and what you want to create. We will then advise you on how to start monetizing your project and we will generate you sales and attract developer interest for your open source project. We will create and manage a bitcoin account for you, where all your finances will live. You will own your private keys.

All of this is free of charge, you will only take a small fee for the transactions generated by us.

Do it yourself (beta)

We offer a dashboard where you can manage your open-source finances. You will be able to pay all the contributors that work for you, and you can generate addresses that you can give your costumers. All the payments made to developers will be automatic once you accept their code into your repository.
